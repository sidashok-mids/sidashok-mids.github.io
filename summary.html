Summary

Find that sweet spot between breaking even and asking too much, or identify a pricepoint for your rare items you want to limit to the market. The bottom axis tells you the average listed price, from low to high, left to right, and the height tells you the total revenue made by a band. Hover over any point to learn what was the asking price, the Average amount over asking and the total revenue for this item. Drop down menu for Select your Genre with the dropdown menu. Clicking on the different color of the bars will highlight that combination, to undo just click on the same point again.
