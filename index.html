W209: Bandcamp Sales Analyzing Genres, Countries, and Bands

Metric: Average Amount over Asking

Average amount over Asking as a metric shows the value of merchandise (Digital or Physical) that a customer pays over the asking price (what the band asks on the Bandcamp website). This is a measure of how willing or passionate a customer is in supporting the artist and can give insights into customer behavior.

Metric: Average Amount Paid

This metric is the raw total amount that a customer pays to obtain the merchandise (Digital or Physical). It shows broad insights about volumes and amount earned for each artist.

Metric: Average Item Price

The Average Item Price metric shows the total sales over the total number of sales to give indications of the relative worth of a piece of merchandise compared to another.
